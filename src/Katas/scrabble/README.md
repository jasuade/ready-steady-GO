Exercise --> SCRABBLE

Given a word, compute the Scrabble score for that word.

Letter Values:

Letter                           Value
------------------------------- | ----
A, E, I, O, U, L, N, R, S, T       1
D, G                               2
B, C, M, P                         3
F, H, V, W, Y                      4
K                                  5
J, X                               8
Q, Z                               10

Example: "cabbage"
-  3 points for C
-  1 point for A, twice
-  3 points for B, twice
-  2 points for G
-  1 point for E
Total = 3 + 2*1 + 2*3 + 2 + 1 = 14

Example: "hello"
- 4 points for H
- 1 point for E
- 1 point for L, twice
- 1 point for O
Total = 4 + 1 + 1*2 + 1 + 8
